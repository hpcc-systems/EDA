Arithmetic Operators

Standard arithmetic operators are supported for use in expressions, listed here in their evaluation precedence:

Division /
Integer DivisionDIV
Modulus Division%
Multiplication*
Addition+
Subtraction-

Division (all three types) by zero (0) results in zero (0)—there is no “divide by zero” error.
